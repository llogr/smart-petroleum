A Fusion of Business and Engineering

SERVICES

Smart Petroleum employs latest thinking, combined with practical financial solutions. In combination with the application of real technologies, Smart Petroleum provides a comprehensive solution to the day-to-day operational needs of rig operators.
Smart Petroleum is built on a foundation of detailed scientific knowledge, combined with practical hands-on experience. Born out of natural intellectual desire, the founders of Smart Petroleum began by developing an expertise in the science and engineering of complex offshore drilling rigs; the challenges, the risks and the solutions.
An interest in the structural aspects of drilling rigs evolved into a passion for solving theoretical problems, these were then applied to the real world of drilling equipment. Smart Petroleum's appetite for asking new and challenging questions, to those involved in the business, has resulted in partnerships, co-operation and knowledge sharing with industry leaders.

Design and Engineering

Smart Petroleum's portfolio of world leading specialists in jack-up drilling manufacture can help advise you on the design and construction of your drilling rig. We can manage your project requirements, including: Project management control:
  • Organisation, scheduling, cost management, HSE, quality control and assurance
  • Project Execution Plan
  • Design Basis and Design Premise
  • Execution & Delivery
  • Client Support

Procurement

Smart Petroleum can assist with your jack-up rig procurement, ensuring you have the correct equipment for your contract as well as providing in-house analysis of the structural integrity of the unit.

HSEQ

Smart Petroleum has a comprehensive HSE and Risk Assessment portfolio, developed from its own experience in the drilling rig industry and in-house expertise together with highly qualified exclusive consultants. Hence we are able to keep you fully abreast of the full range of international safety regulations.
